Why Web Development?

Previously I worked in the recruitment industry and for the past 5 years I worked as a research associate. I felt secure at my job but I also had no growth. I knew that I needed a career change. Over 3 years ago, I looked into coding and knew that learning web development could help me start a career that I could enjoy. At first I started with online learning and then luckily found some people to pair-program with. I learned a great amount from those individuals and gained a lot of confidence after 2 years. I soon realized that I had waited long enough to make a change and I decided to enroll in Coding Temple to begin my new career path.
